Years of Trust

Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Vision Care

Rajesh Optical is a trusted destination for premium eyewear and professional vision care services. With years of dedication in the optical industry, we focus on providing the perfect combination of style, comfort, and clarity.

Our mission is to ensure every customer experiences the best vision care with modern testing equipment, quality lenses, and stylish frames that suit different personalities and lifestyles.

We believe eyewear is more than just a necessity — it is an important part of personal style and confidence, which is why we carefully curate collections that combine fashion and functionality.

Eye Care

Advanced Eye Care Technology

Our eye testing services are performed using modern computerized diagnostic systems that help detect vision problems accurately.

We assist customers in identifying refractive errors, selecting correct lens prescriptions, and choosing lenses that improve visual comfort.

Our professionals provide expert consultation for progressive lenses, anti-glare lenses, blue light protection glasses, and customized vision solutions.

Innovation

Our Advancements in Eyewear

Rajesh Optical continuously adopts the latest advancements in optical technology to ensure customers receive high-quality eyewear solutions.

We provide advanced lenses including high-index lenses, UV protection lenses, anti-reflective coated lenses, and blue light filtering glasses.

Our frame collections include titanium frames, fiber frames, lightweight metal frames, and flexible designs that provide durability and superior comfort.

Equipment

Technology & Equipment We Use

We use advanced digital Auto Refractometers, Digital Vision Analyzers, Lensometers, and automated lens edging machines to ensure perfect lens fitting and alignment.

Our computerized systems help in accurate pupil distance measurement and high precision lens cutting.

By combining professional expertise with modern technology, we deliver superior optical care for every customer.
